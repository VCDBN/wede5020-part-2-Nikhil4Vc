~Our objectives~


Donating old technology to the less fortunate or trash? More then a third of the worlds population do not have access to the internet due to a lack of electronic resources. These people can not afford computers and cell phones so they are unable to grasp the advantages of technology. Help other by donating your old electronic devices, from mobile devices to computers anything is well appreciated. We will ensure that every device donated reaches a person in need!. A computer can be a political tool that divides communities, instead lets use it to unite the world.


Your help is most appreciated. We will be on the look out for people who enjoy teaching and educating other on how to use these technological devices. This is one of our projects that will help close the gap of the digital divide by educating others and teaching the basics of the internet. We encourage the youth to volunteer and lead these projects. When people know how to use various devices it opens up a whole new world for them. People would become more social and be able to gain access to more infomation and job opportunities.


Do you love your planet? Cause we sure do!. We are commited into doing everything we can to save the environment. Each year more them 100 million phones are discarded, so we would love if you could give your old devices that are not at use or no longer working to us instead of disposing the wrong way. Acids and chemicals from old phone batteries can leak and damage its surroundings endangering lives of plants and animals, not to mention other materials used to make phones. Cause always remember that the blurred image around your phone is called life.
